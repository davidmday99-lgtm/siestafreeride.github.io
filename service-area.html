Our Service Area

Siesta Free Ride provides free transportation across Siesta Key and surrounding areas. From beach runs and off-key grocery trips to Sarasota, Tampa, Punta Gorda, and Ft. Myers airport transfers—we've got you covered. Tips only!

📍 Interactive map showing our full service coverage area

🏝️ Primary Service Area: Siesta Key

Our core service area covers all of Siesta Key—from Turtle Beach on the south end to Siesta Key Village on the north. This includes:

🏖️ Siesta Beach 🐢 Turtle Beach 🏘️ Siesta Key Village 🌴 Crescent Beach 🏠 Mid-Key Residential 🌅 Point of Rocks ✈️ Sarasota / SRQ Airport ✈️ Tampa Airport ✈️ Punta Gorda Airport ✈️ Ft. Myers Airport 🍺 Gulf Gate Bars & Restaurants 🛒 Off-Key Grocery Stores

Within Siesta Key: All rides are FREE (tips appreciated!). Whether you need a beach drop-off, restaurant run, or just getting back to your rental—we're here.
