Siesta Key Events Today - Things to Do This Week

From Sunday drum circles to world-class sandcastle competitions - here's what's happening on the Key. We update this regularly.

📅 Weekly Recurring Events

🏄 Sunday Drum Circle

Every Sunday • Sunset • Siesta Beach

Siesta Key's most iconic tradition. Hundreds of people gather on the beach with drums, instruments, and dance. Arrive 30-60 minutes before sunset to grab a spot. Free to attend, free to join. One of the top things to do in all of Sarasota.

🌽 Siesta Key Farmers Market

Every Sunday • 8am - 1pm • Siesta Key Village

Fresh local produce, baked goods, honey, handmade crafts, and live music. Located in the Village along Ocean Boulevard. A great way to start your Sunday before the beach. Runs year-round, rain or shine.

🎶 Live Music Nights

Thursday - Sunday • Various times • Village bars

SKOB, Daiquiri Deck, Gilligan's, and Beach Club all have live music throughout the week. Thursday through Sunday nights are the most active. No cover at most spots except Beach Club on peak weekends.

🌅 Sunset at Point of Rocks

Every evening • Sunset • Crescent Beach

A local favorite for watching the sun go down. The rocky outcrop creates beautiful photo opportunities. Less crowded than the main beach. Bring a towel and a drink (non-alcoholic on the beach!). Also great for snorkeling during the day.

🗓 Monthly Highlights

January - March

Peak season. Art festivals in downtown Sarasota, boat shows, and the Sarasota Film Festival. Snowbird season means the Village is bustling every night. Book dinner reservations early.

April - June

Spring break winds down, summer ramps up. Turtle nesting season begins (May). Offshore fishing heats up. Memorial Day weekend is the unofficial start of summer - expect big crowds.

July - September

Summer heat, afternoon thunderstorms, and the quietest tourist period. 4th of July fireworks over Sarasota Bay are spectacular. Great time for locals to enjoy the Key without the crowds.

October

Shoulder season begins. Perfect weather returns. Halloween events in the Village. Siesta Key restaurants offer seasonal specials to attract diners. One of the best months to visit.

November

Crystal Classic Master Sandcastle Competition - the premier event on Siesta Key. World-class sculptors create massive sand art installations on the beach. Free to watch. Tens of thousands attend. A must-see.

December

Holiday season kicks in. Siesta Key Village holiday decorations, special dinner menus, and New Year's Eve celebrations at the bars. Snowbird season returns mid-month. Book early.

🎉 Major Annual Events

🏖 Siesta Key Crystal Classic

November • Siesta Beach • Free admission

The premier sand sculpting competition in the southeastern United States. Professional and amateur sculptors create jaw-dropping works of art from Siesta Key's famous quartz sand. Multiple day event with food vendors, live music, and art shows. Don't miss it.

🍣 Sarasota Seafood Festival

March • Sarasota Bayfront • Ticketed

Fresh Gulf seafood, craft beer, live music, and cooking demonstrations. Located on the Sarasota bayfront - a quick ride from the Key. Vendors serve everything from stone crab claws to gator bites. One of the biggest food festivals in Southwest Florida.

🎨 Sarasota Art Festival

February • Downtown Sarasota • Free admission

One of the top-ranked art festivals in the country. Over 200 artists exhibit painting, sculpture, photography, glass, and mixed media. Held along Main Street and Palm Avenue. Food and drinks available. Great day trip from the Key.

🎆 4th of July Fireworks

July 4th • Sarasota Bay • Free

Massive fireworks display over Sarasota Bay visible from multiple locations. Watch from Bayfront Park, the bridges, or find a spot on the Key facing the mainland. The drum circle on the closest Sunday often draws record crowds.
