Daily Update - June 3, 2026

June 3, 2026•3 min read

Good morning, Siesta Key! It's Wednesday, June 3, 2026—almost time for the weekend festival! Here's your daily update from Siesta Free Ride with weather, beach conditions, and festival prep info.

Wednesday Pre-Festival Weather

☀️ Weather

Mostly sunny this morning at 80°F with 72% humidity and light E breezes at 3 mph. Another hot summer day brewing. High reaches 91°F—the heat is building. Afternoon storms possible 2–4 PM; then clearing for evening. Evening low drops to 75°F. Sunrise at 6:17 AM; sunset at 8:35 PM.

🌊 Water & Beach Conditions

Gulf water at 84°F—warm and inviting! Swells 1-2 feet with good visibility. The water is perfect for swimming and water sports. The beach will be moderately busy as families plan their festival weekend.

🎉 Siesta Key Beach Festival This Weekend!

June 4–6 (Thursday–Saturday) — Mark your calendars! The annual Siesta Key Beach Festival returns with:

  • Live Music: Local and regional bands performing all weekend
  • Food Vendors: Beach eats, fresh seafood, tropical drinks
  • Sand Sculpture Competition: Incredible artistry on Friday afternoon
  • Family Activities: Games, contests, beach Olympics
  • Craft Booths: Local artists and vendors
  • Sunset Celebrations: Special evening events each night

Hours: Thursday & Friday 4 PM–10 PM; Saturday 10 AM–10 PM

Location: Main Beach Pavilion & surrounding area

🎯 Festival Tips & Planning

When to Go:

  • Thursday: Less crowded, perfect for families with kids
  • Friday: Mid-week energy, sand sculpture competition at 2 PM
  • Saturday: Most crowded, peak energy, best entertainment lineup

What to Bring:

  • Sunscreen (SPF 50+) and reapply frequently
  • Hat and sunglasses
  • Cash and cards for vendors
  • Water and electrolyte drinks
  • Comfortable shoes (you'll be walking)
  • Light jacket for evening (cools down after sunset)

Parking: Arrive before 5 PM Thursday/Friday, or before 9 AM Saturday for better parking. Otherwise, use shuttle parking or our transportation service.

📅 Wednesday Preparation

Tonight: Farmers Market in Siesta Key Village 3 PM–sunset. Great spot for fresh produce and evening shopping.

Tomorrow: Siesta Sunset Yoga at Crescent Beach 6:30 PM—stretch before the festival weekend!
