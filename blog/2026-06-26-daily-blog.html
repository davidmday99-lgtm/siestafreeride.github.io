👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 Family Activities

Siesta Key is perfect for families. Here are the best activities for all ages today:

🏰 Sandcastle Building

Siesta's fine quartz sand molds beautifully. The north end near Crescent Beach is less crowded for castle projects.

🏊‍♂️ Shallow Water Play

The beach slopes gently. Water is only 2-3 feet deep 100 feet out—perfect for toddlers and beginner swimmers under calm conditions.

🎣 Seashell Hunting

June's warm water brings colorful shells and small sea creatures. Search tide pools at Point of Rocks for hermit crabs and small fish.

🎯 Beach Games

Volleyball, frisbee, paddleball, beach balls—the soft sand is perfect and forgiving for all skill levels.

📸 Family Photos

Morning (7-9 AM) and evening (6:30-8:30 PM) light is perfect. Golden hour means your family portraits will look magazine-ready!

🍪 Pack Smart

Bring: sunscreen, water water water, healthy snacks (fruit, granola), small toys/beach games, towels, and a beach bag. Avoid sugary drinks and heavy foods—they make beach time uncomfortable in the heat.
