Daily Update - April 7, 2026

April 7, 2026•3 min read

Happy Tuesday, Siesta Key! Easter weekend has wound down and the island is settling into a quieter mid-week rhythm. That means fewer crowds, easier parking, and plenty of white quartz sand all to yourself. Here's everything you need to know for today.

Today's Weather

Tuesday is shaping up to be a breezy but pleasant day on Siesta Key. We're seeing partly cloudy skies with a current temperature of 70°F and humidity around 73%. A northeast wind is blowing at about 22 mph, keeping things feeling comfortable with a slight coastal edge. Today's high should hit around 73°F with a low near 67°F overnight. Sunrise was at 7:14 AM and sunset is at 7:51 PM.

The stronger winds make this a great day for kite flying on Siesta Beach or paddleboarding in the calmer bay-side waters. The Gulf side may be a bit choppy in the morning, so plan your beach time for the afternoon if you want smoother conditions.

What's Happening in Siesta Key

Siesta Key Farmers Market — Every Sunday from 8 AM to 1 PM at the Siesta Key Village. After the Easter rush, this week's market will have fresh seasonal produce, baked goods from local bakeries, live acoustic music, and handmade crafts. It's one of the best ways to experience the island's community spirit.

Beachcombing at Low Tide — The breezy conditions often bring interesting shells and sea glass to the shoreline, especially on the north end of Siesta Beach. Mid-week is the perfect time for a quiet walk—fewer people and more treasures to discover.

Mid-Week Dining in the Village — Tuesday nights are a great secret for locals and visitors who want to skip the weekend wait lines. Restaurants like The Hub Baja Grill, Gilligan's Bar & Grill, and The Village Table are more relaxed than usual. Ask about happy-hour specials—many village spots offer them mid-week to keep things lively.

Looking Ahead: This Weekend — The April breeze is warming up. By the weekend, temperatures are forecast to climb into the upper 70s, making it ideal for boat trips, sunset cruises, and beach lounging. Start thinking about your plans now.
