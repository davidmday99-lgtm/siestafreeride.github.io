Festival Day #1 — Let's Go!

Sunny and warm this morning at 81°F with 70% humidity and light E breezes at 2 mph. A gorgeous day for the festival kickoff! High reaches 89°F—warm but not brutal. 🎯 Festival Day Tips

Dress for Success:

  • Comfortable walking shoes (you'll be on sand and pavement)
  • Hat and sunglasses (all-day sun exposure)
  • Light, breathable clothing
  • Light jacket for evening (beach breezes cool down)

Hydration & Nutrition:

  • Bring a reusable water bottle and refill stations available
  • Tons of food vendors—try local specialties
  • Budget $15–25 per person for food/drinks

Sun Protection:

  • SPF 50+ sunscreen—reapply every 2 hours
  • Lip balm with SPF
  • Sunglasses with UV protection
