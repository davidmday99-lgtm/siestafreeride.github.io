Heat index of 112°F is dangerous. Stay hydrated, take breaks in shade or A/C, and watch for signs of heat exhaustion. Children and elderly are especially vulnerable.

Sunday on Siesta Key means families wrapping up weekend vacations, locals enjoying their day off, and that special end-of-weekend energy in the Village. The beach will be busy early as people try to beat the worst of the heat, then picks back up for sunset hours.

Pro tip for today: Hit the beach early (before 10 AM) or late (after 5 PM). The midday heat is genuinely dangerous for extended outdoor exposure. Many visitors use the hottest hours for lunch in an air-conditioned Village restaurant, shopping, or a siesta back at their rental.
