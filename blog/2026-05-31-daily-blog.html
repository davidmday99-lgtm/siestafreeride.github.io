Daily Update - May 31, 2026

May 31, 2026•3 min read

Good morning, Siesta Key! It's Sunday, May 31, 2026—the final day of an incredible holiday weekend. Here's your daily update from Siesta Free Ride with weather, beach conditions, and a gentle reminder to rest up before June.

Sunday's Relaxed Beach Day

⛅ Weather

Partly cloudy this morning at 77°F with 68% humidity and light ENE breezes at 6 mph. A comfortable Sunday. High reaches 88°F—still beautiful but slightly less intense than yesterday. Evening low dips to 72°F. Sunrise at 6:17 AM; sunset at 8:33 PM.

🌊 Water Conditions

Gulf water is a perfect 82°F with calm 1-2 foot swells. Excellent for swimming, paddleboarding, and floating. The beach will still be busy with Sunday funday crowds, but less intense than the past two days.

🏖️ Sunday Beach Vibes

This is the perfect day to slow down and soak it all in. Whether you arrive early for a quiet morning swim or spend a lazy afternoon with family and friends, Sunday feels more relaxed than the holiday rush.

Evening Plans? The sunset tonight will be stunning—arrive at the beach by 8:15 PM for the full show. Bring a light blanket and someone special.

📅 Sunday Activities

Sunday Morning Beach Yoga at Crescent Beach, 8 AM. Sunrise salutations and peaceful stretching to ease into the day. All levels welcome.

Siesta Key Farmers Market (last weekend market before June) runs 8 AM–1 PM with local vendors, live music, and peak season produce.

Sunday Drum Circle at Sunset (every Sunday at dusk on Siesta Beach). Join the community for an hour of rhythm and connection. Bring your own drums or just enjoy the beat.

🎒 Weekend Recap & Recovery

If you're heading back home or returning to the routine tomorrow, take today to:

  • Relax and hydrate
  • Pack up your beach gear
  • Enjoy one last meal at a favorite restaurant
  • Watch the sunset without rushing
  • Reflect on a wonderful holiday weekend

📝 Welcome to June!

Tomorrow brings June 1st—the official start of summer and the beginning of peak season on Siesta Key. If you're staying through the month, expect:

  • Warmer water (83–84°F)
  • More consistent sunshine
  • Busier beaches and restaurants
  • Afternoon thunderstorm season beginning
  • Spectacular summer sunsets at 8:34 PM+
