Getting Around Siesta Key

Planning your day at Siesta Key? One of the best-kept secrets for visitors is understanding how to get around in Siesta Key efficiently. Whether you're staying in a nearby resort or exploring the area for the day, there are several convenient options to consider.

For those interested in free rides on Siesta Key, ask your hotel concierge about complementary shuttle services. Many beachfront properties offer free shuttle service to and from the public beach areas and shopping districts. This is an excellent way to avoid parking hassles and enjoy the scenic routes around the island.

Local Tips for getting around Siesta Key:

  • Beach Parking: Arrive early to secure a spot near Siesta Beach Public Park, or utilize the beachfront metered parking on Midnight Pass Road
  • Trolleys & Shuttles: Check with your accommodation about complimentary shuttle services—many hotels offer this perk
  • Biking: Rent a bike and explore the scenic pathways along Siesta Key Boulevard
  • Walking: The beach village is very walkable, with shops and restaurants just steps from the sand
  • Rideshare: Apps like Uber and Lyft serve the area for quick trips around the key
