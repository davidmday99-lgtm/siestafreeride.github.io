🌞 Weather & Water Conditions

Late May in Siesta Key is pure paradise. Expect:

  • High Temperature: 87°F with gentle breezes (10-12 mph from the southeast)
  • Water Temperature: 84°F — warm enough for comfortable swimming and water activities
  • Sunshine: Clear skies all day with minimal clouds. UV index is high, so sunscreen is essential!
  • Beach Conditions: Gentle 2-3 foot swells, perfect for casual swimmers and paddle-boarders
  • Tides: High tide at 12:45 PM and 1:00 AM tomorrow. Low tide at 7:15 AM and 7:30 PM

This is textbook Florida beach weather — the kind of day you moved to Siesta Key for.
