Today at Siesta Key

May 30 brings true tropical conditions to Siesta Key! With temperatures climbing to a warm 86°F and increasing humidity, this feels like authentic Florida summer weather. The Gulf water has warmed to a comfortable 82°F—perfect for cooling off from the heat. Light winds of 6-9 mph provide some relief from the humidity while keeping water conditions manageable for all activities.

The partly cloudy skies offer welcome breaks from the intense sun, creating a more comfortable beach experience than completely clear skies would provide. This type of weather is typical for late May in Florida and signals the transition into full summer mode.

🏖️ Water & Beach Conditions

At 82°F, the Gulf water provides the perfect antidote to the warm air temperature—cool enough to feel refreshing but warm enough for extended swimming without getting cold. The light winds create gentle 2-3 foot waves, ideal for body surfing and water play. Siesta Key's quartz sand remains relatively cool underfoot, though it will feel warm during peak afternoon hours.

Pro Tip: Hydration is absolutely critical today! The combination of heat, humidity, sun, and salt water creates perfect conditions for dehydration. Bring at least 2-3 liters of water per person and consider electrolyte supplements. Plan for frequent shade breaks and avoid prolonged sun exposure during peak hours (11 AM - 3 PM).

Sunset tonight is at 8:17 PM, providing a long day of beach time. Water clarity remains good, though slightly reduced from earlier in the week due to increased activity and minor wave action. The warm water temperature makes evening swims particularly pleasant as the air cools after sunset.

🎯 Things to Do Today

Water Activities

Swimming and floating are essential activities today, with the 82°F water providing welcome relief from the heat. The gentle waves are perfect for body surfing, boogie boarding, or learning to skimboard. Paddleboarding and kayaking are best done in the early morning or late afternoon to avoid the strongest heat.

Beach Relaxation

Set up camp under substantial shade—umbrellas, pop-up tents, or natural dune vegetation. The partly cloudy conditions make beach reading and relaxation more comfortable than in full sun. Beach games are best scheduled for early morning or evening when temperatures are more moderate.

Local Events & Activities

Check for any Memorial Day weekend events still happening in Siesta Key Village—many businesses extend special activities through the weekend. Indoor activities like visiting local art galleries or shopping in air-conditioned stores provide welcome breaks from the heat. Many restaurants offer special tropical drinks and summer menus perfect for hot, humid days.

🍽️ Dining & Refreshments

Cold, refreshing beverages and light meals are the order of the day. Look for restaurants offering frozen cocktails, fresh fruit smoothies, cold salads, and seafood ceviche.
