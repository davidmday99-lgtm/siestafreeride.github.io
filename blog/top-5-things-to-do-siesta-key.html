Top 5 Things to Do in Siesta Key This Weekend

April 1, 2026 • 5 min read

Siesta Key isn't just another Florida beach town—it's a weekend paradise waiting to be explored. Whether you're visiting for the first time or you're a seasoned local looking for fresh ideas, here are our top 5 picks for an unforgettable weekend.

1. Sunset Beach Stroll at Siesta Beach

There's a reason Siesta Beach consistently ranks among America's best beaches. The quartz‑crystal sand stays cool underfoot even on the hottest days, and the sunset views over the Gulf are nothing short of magical. Grab a blanket, pack a picnic, and arrive about 45 minutes before sunset for the full experience. Pro tip: parking fills up quickly on weekends, so consider using our ride service and let us handle the drop‑off and pick‑up.

2. Explore Siesta Key Village

The Village is the heart of Siesta Key's dining and nightlife scene. Start with breakfast at Another Broken Egg Cafe, wander through the local boutiques, then settle in for live music at The Hub Baja Grill in the afternoon. Don't miss the fresh seafood at The Cottage—their grouper sandwich is legendary. Best of all, everything is within walking distance once you're there.

3. Kayak Through the Mangroves

For a change of pace from the beach, head to nearby Point of Rocks or Turtle Beach for guided kayak tours through serene mangrove tunnels. You'll spot herons, ospreys, and if you're lucky, manatees. Several local outfitters offer rentals and guided tours—most include all equipment and a brief safety lesson. It's a perfect 2‑3 hour adventure that feels worlds away from the mainland.

4. Dinner with a View at The Speakeasy

Perched on the second floor overlooking the Village, The Speakeasy offers craft cocktails and a modern American menu in a stylish, relaxed setting. Reserve a table on the balcony for people‑watching as the evening comes alive below. Their happy hour (4‑6 PM) features discounted appetizers and drinks—a great way to start your evening before moving on to other Village hotspots.

5. Sunday Morning Farmers Market

Every Sunday from 8 AM to 1 PM, the Siesta Key Farmers Market sets up in Davidson Plaza. You'll find local honey, fresh‑baked pastries, handmade jewelry, and live acoustic music. It's the perfect low‑key way to wrap up your weekend before heading home. Pick up some local treats for the road—you'll thank yourself later.
