🏖️ Beach Conditions: Ideal for All Activities

Wednesday's partly cloudy skies offer natural sun breaks while keeping temperatures comfortable. The Gulf water sits at a pleasant 74°F with waves under 2 feet—perfect conditions for:

  • Swimming: Warm, calm waters with excellent visibility
  • Shell Collecting: Gentle waves bring fresh treasures to shore
  • Beach Volleyball: Light winds won't interfere with your game
  • Photography: Dramatic clouds create stunning beach backdrops
  • Fishing: Calm surf makes wade fishing very productive

📍 Local Happening: Siesta Key Farmers Market

Don't miss the Siesta Key Farmers Market today from 3-7 PM at Siesta Key Village! This weekly market features local produce, artisan crafts, and food vendors. It's the perfect way to experience authentic island culture while enjoying the beautiful weather.

Pro tip: Grab fresh fruit for your beach picnic or browse local art while waiting for the golden hour to hit the sand.

☀️ Sun Safety for High UV Day

With a UV index of 9, today requires serious sun protection:

  • Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen 30 minutes before sun exposure
  • Reapply every 2 hours, even on cloudy periods
  • Seek shade between 11 AM - 3 PM when UV is strongest
  • Wear a wide-brimmed hat and UV-blocking sunglasses
  • Stay hydrated—bring extra water for the warmer temperatures

🌅 Perfect Timing for Your Day

With over 12.5 hours of daylight, you have plenty of time to enjoy everything Siesta Key offers. Early morning (8-10 AM) is perfect for peaceful beach walks, midday for active water sports, and late afternoon for the farmers market before ending with a stunning sunset.
