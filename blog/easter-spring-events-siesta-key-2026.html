Easter & Spring Events in Siesta Key 2026

April 2, 2026 • 6 min read

Spring is in full bloom on Siesta Key and Easter 2026 (April 5) brings a weekend of family-friendly activities, beach gatherings, and special events. Whether you're visiting for the holiday or enjoying a local spring break, here's what's happening around the island.

Easter Egg Hunts & Family Fun

Siesta Key Village Easter Egg Hunt — Saturday, April 4, 10 AM at Davidson Plaza. The Village Association hosts a massive egg hunt with thousands of candy-filled eggs, plus photos with the Easter Bunny. Bring your own basket; the event is free and open to all ages.

Sunset Beach Easter Sunrise Service — Sunday, April 5, 6:30 AM at Siesta Beach (near Pavilion 1). Start your Easter morning with a non-denominational sunrise service over the Gulf. Bring a blanket and experience the beauty of dawn on world-famous quartz sand.

Spring Festivals & Markets

Siesta Key Farmers Market — Every Sunday 8 AM–1 PM. The market features local produce, handmade crafts, live acoustic music, and fresh baked goods. A perfect way to spend a lazy Sunday morning before heading to the beach.

Art Walk Siesta Key — First Friday of the month (April 4, 5–8 PM). Local artists set up throughout the Village with paintings, pottery, and photography. Many galleries extend hours and offer refreshments.

Beach & Outdoor Activities

April weather in Siesta Key is ideal: average highs around 82°F, low humidity, and minimal rain. The Gulf water temperature climbs into the comfortable 70s. Perfect for:

  • Beach volleyball — Courts available at Siesta Beach (free, first-come basis)
  • Kayak & paddleboard rentals — Numerous operators at Turtle Beach and near the village; many offer Easter weekend discounts
  • Fishing charters — Book your spot for inshore or deep-sea excursions; April is excellent for snook, redfish, and trout

Dining Specials & Holiday Menus

Many Village restaurants feature special Easter brunch and dinner menus:

  • The Cottage — Easter seafood platter (reservations required)
  • The Hub Baja Grill — Easter brunch buffet with live music
  • The Speakeasy — Craft cocktails and small plates Easter evening

Need a Ride?
