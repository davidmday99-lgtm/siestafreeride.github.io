🏖️ Beach Conditions

Thursday brings typical Florida summer weather: 90°F temperatures and a 40% chance of afternoon thunderstorms. This is actually the norm for late June—brief, intense thunderstorms roll through in the afternoon, typically 2-4 PM, and clear just as quickly.

The morning is excellent for beach time! Head out early, enjoy sunrise to midday, then retreat to air-conditioned dining or indoor activities before the afternoon pop-up storms arrive. The storms cool things down beautifully and rarely last more than 30-45 minutes.

⚡ Afternoon Storm Alert

Possible brief thunderstorms 2-4 PM. Beach is typically cleared by 3 PM during storm season. Plan beach time 6-11 AM. Storms clear it up for evening activities!

🌈 Silver Lining

After the afternoon storm, expect clearing skies, cooler temps, and often the most spectacular sunset colors of the week!

🍽️ Siesta Key Village Dining

With a potential afternoon storm, today is perfect for village dining! Enjoy breakfast and morning beach time, then head to Siesta Key Village for lunch and a leisurely afternoon of eating, shopping, and exploring—all indoors or under covered patios.

Siesta Key Village is packed with excellent restaurants, casual eateries, and specialty shops. Here's what to hit:

The Hub Restaurant & Bar

Local favorite with craft cocktails, fresh seafood, and waterfront views. Perfect for lunch or dinner.

Ophelia's On The Bay

Fine dining with sunset views (perfect for 8 PM after storms clear). Seafood-focused with elegant presentation.

Siesta Fiesta

Casual Mexican fare, great margaritas, and lively vibe. Family-friendly and budget-conscious.

Beach Bistro

Sophisticated American cuisine. Romantic spot for couples, quiet enough for conversation.

Caroline Street Antique Shops

Browse vintage treasures, local artist galleries, and unique boutiques while you wait for weather to clear.

💡 Village Dining Strategy

Early lunch (11-12 PM) before crowds and potential storms. Browse shops 1-3 PM while thunderstorms roll through inland. Late dinner after 7 PM for twilight/sunset dining with cleared skies!
