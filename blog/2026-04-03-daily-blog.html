Daily Update - April 3, 2026

April 3, 2026•3 min read

Good morning, Siesta Key! It's Friday, April 3, 2026—the start of a beautiful Easter weekend. Here’s your daily update from Siesta Free Ride with everything you need to know about today’s weather, local happenings, and transportation tips.

Today's Weather & Events

🌤️ Weather

This morning we’re looking at partly cloudy skies with a comfortable 82°F (feels like 88°F). Humidity is at 55% with a light ESE breeze around 13 mph. Today’s high will reach 92°F—perfect for beach time—and tonight’s low drops to 69°F. Sunrise was at 7:19 AM; sunset is at 7:49 PM.

🎉 What's Happening

Easter Weekend is here! The annual Siesta Key Village Easter Egg Hunt is tomorrow (Saturday, April 4) at 10 AM in Davidson Plaza. Bring your own basket—thousands of candy‑filled eggs, plus photos with the Easter Bunny.

Sunrise Service on Siesta Beach Sunday, April 5 at 6:30 AM near Pavilion 1. A non‑denominational service overlooking the Gulf—bring a blanket and welcome Easter morning on our famous quartz sand.

Siesta Key Farmers Market runs every Sunday 8 AM–1 PM with local produce, crafts, live music, and baked goods.

🚗 Transportation Tip

With holiday crowds and limited parking, pre‑booking your ride is the smart move. Siesta Free Ride operates 7 AM–11 PM daily and can take you anywhere on the island, to/from SRQ Airport, or even to Tampa (TPA). Planning ahead guarantees you a stress‑free start to your Easter celebrations.
